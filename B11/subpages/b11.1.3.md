Current MLC records for print monographs contain the following values:
Encoding level (Ldr/7) 7
906 $d 4
050 MLC shelf number beginning with “MLC”
Non-print monographic resources may have a different value in the 050 field, such as “Microfilm”
or “Microfiche.”  See the MARC 21 Fields Used in Serial Records section of DCM *Z* 1 for information
about 042 and 050 values in MLC serial records.
Some older copy cataloging records contain MLC shelf numbers in field 050, but the encoding
level is blank.
