Remove all slips from resources cataloged as MLC except :
• Binding Instructions for BCCD
• Materials Routing (for books with accompanying sound recordings or videorecordings).
The Binding Instructions for BCCD slip is used to flag materials designated as “physical aspects”
resources for review by BCCD before the resources are sent to CALM see DCM B11.7.4 for instructions
on filling out the slip.
The Materials Routing slip is used to send books with accompanying sound recordings or
videorecordings to MBAS.
