Cataloger's notes may be added, changed, or deleted just as any other field. Generally,
correct or delete 952 fields only when they are wrong or misleading. Do **not** correct a record
solely to delete 952 fields made under previous guidelines but that would not be made under the
present ones. Such notes may, however, be deleted if correcting a record for another reason. In
general, delete 952 fields indicating the parts added when the last volume of a collected set
record is added and the record is closed.
