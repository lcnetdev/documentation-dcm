Confine cataloger's notes to information that

1. documents substantive decisions;
2. records information especially verified (e.g., by telephone; by ordering item
from shelf);
3. records the results of a special investigation (to prevent a repeat of the
investigation should the same question be raised again);
4. records specific kinds of information called for in other DCMs or as in DCM

B9.3.2.2 below.
