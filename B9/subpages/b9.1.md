These guidelines apply to monograph catalogers only. Use the 952 field (Cataloger's
Permanent Note) to record information relevant to the cataloging process itself. The 952 field is
local to LC. It is not contained in records distributed through the MARC Distribution Service. It
is displayed in the Cataloging Module of the ILS and in the MARC view of the OPAC.  The
field is repeatable and is available for use in both bibliographic and authority records.
