In contrast to previous practice, items undergoing CIP verification/upgrade now also undergo
charging upon receipt ("bk. rec'd. [code] [date]") as well as at the time of actual
verification/upgrade ("CIP ver. [code] [date]"). Note that the requirement to charge upon receipt
does not mean that a separate charging stage *per se* is mandated.  If items can be given directly
to staff for CIP verification/upgrade to begin the same day, then the charge can be done when
work on them begins. If work on an item does not begin that day, then do the charge the next
working day.
If an item goes to Dewey because of a red II, discharge to Dewey after CIP
verification/upgrade ("CIP ver.; to DDC [code] [date]") and forward the item.
