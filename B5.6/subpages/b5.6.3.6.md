New full cataloging for children's materials (works primarily for children and young adults) which
are *not in scope for full cataloging by the Children's Literature team* is done first in the relevant
team (including shelflisting) and is then forwarded to the Children's Literature team. The
forwarding team discharges and logs out (as fully completed work). The Children's Literature
team charges and logs in. Upon completion of its work the team discharges, if appropriate, to
decimal classification ("to DDC [date]") and in all cases logs out (AC only).
