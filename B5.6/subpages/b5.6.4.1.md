Chart 3 depicts the three different workflows for the CIP verification/upgrade process (item
received, checked against record, record adjusted to reflect item and then upgraded from the CIP
state to a full one), namely:

1. that done in the CIP Division itself, which entails most of the routine CIP
verification/upgrade exclusive of red IIs, items in class M, and items that are computer
files and microforms; CIP Division determines the disposition of items between CIP
Division and the regular cataloging divisions and, for the latter, the disposition of items
among them;
2. that done by senior technicians in the regular cataloging teams as workload permits and
with assistance from descriptive catalogers for necessary authority work as needed; this
workflow is shown on the lower right side of the chart;
3. that done by descriptive catalogers; this workflow is shown on the lower left side of the
chart.
