Process duplicates as follows:

a. send the duplicate to the duplicate shelf in the division sorting/distribution area for a
decision by the appropriate selection official on whether to keep or not;
b. if the decision is to keep
1. and the bibliographic record contains a completed call number, send the duplicate to
the appropriate team to be added to the collections (the item is shelflisted and then
sent for binding/labelling);
2. but the bibliographic record for copy 1 is still in process, send the duplicate to the last
955 charging destination for copy 1; that team then matches the duplicate with copy 1
and both are shelflisted together when cataloging is completed;
c. if the decision is to discard, send the duplicate to the Exchange and Gift Division.
