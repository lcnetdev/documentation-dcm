Chart 2 covers both cataloging that is done solely within one team and cross-team cataloging.
In some cases cross-team cataloging is intentionally used to accommodate the disposition of
language and subject skills or other staffing considerations. In other cases, an item is discovered
to be out of subject scope only after the descriptive aspect is completed. The broken line in the
middle of Chart 2 illustrates the fact that an item may move from one team to another at this
point.
The team completing the descriptive aspect discharges and logs out (indicating partial
completion). The team receiving the item charges and logs in (as subject aspect only) followed
by distribution to appropriate staff for completing cataloging. The details of discharging and
logging out and charging and logging in are at the discretion of each team subject to general
guidelines stated in DCM B5.6.3.3 (Appendix 1).
