The statistics area of the slip is used to record auxiliary statistics for authority work and the
LC Database file maintenance. *All numbers are recorded as arabic numerals.* Descriptive
catalogers record the number of name authorities made, series authorities made, and name/series
authorities modified. Subject catalogers record the number of subject authorities made, subject
authorities modified, class numbers established, and class numbers changed. Technicians record
the number of items reshelflisted. Any cataloger or technician records information for
bibliographic records changed, as necessary.
