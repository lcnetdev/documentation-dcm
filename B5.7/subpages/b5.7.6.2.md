*Top area:* Ensures that the data at the top of the slip are correct. For teams receiving items
not yet under initial bibliographic control, this may involve writing in the RID. The priority and
language may also need to be corrected manually on the slip.
*Statistics area:* Records in **arabic** numerals the number of name authorities made/ changed
and the number of bibliographic records changed.
*Completion area:*

a. records the date of completion of the descriptive aspect;
b. indicates, as necessary, the appropriate source category (see DCM B5.7.5.3.b above)
for items receiving descriptive cataloging only, new input from ONC/PREMARC
(note that the new input procedure is stimulated by added volume activity and
therefore the item involved will have been logged-in as such), MLC, and collection
level cataloging;
c. indicates, as necessary, the activity completed;
d. fills out a Materials Routing slip to accompany the item being forwarded; provides, as
necessary, on that slip collected set and other information for the person who will be
doing the subject aspect; NOTE: Ser Rec staff forwarding material as the result of
doing normal descriptive-only cataloging of serials use instead a Serials Work
Sheet/Routing Notice (form 6-69i);
e. forwards the item, and routes the STARS Slip for log-out according to team practice.
