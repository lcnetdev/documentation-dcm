Cross-team cataloging occurs when all the aspects of cataloging cannot be completed within
the same team; in these instances each team logs the item in and out of its own STARS database.
When an item is forwarded out of a team after cataloging is partially completed, data which will
be needed for the later stages of cataloging (e.g., series treatment, name headings provided for
the subject aspect) are recorded on a Materials Routing slip, which travels with the item to the
next team. The original team uses information from the original STARS Slip to log the item out
and then discards the slip. The receiving team logs the item into its own STARS database and in
most cases prints a new STARS Slip.
