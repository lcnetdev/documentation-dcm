Log items out of the team's STARS database by invoking either the Log Item Out screen or
the Log Item In/Out screen. Use the latter when:

1. the STARS Slip shows manual corrections to printed fields, e.g. a miskeyed RID or
incorrect Language value; such corrections to a STARS database can only be made
by invoking the Log Item In/Out screen;
2. the deferred log-in model is used;
3. the cataloging activity does not require a STARS Slip to be generated; such activities
are determined by individual division/team policy and typically include shelflisting
The routines used to administer logging out are at the discretion of the team. Most teams
will probably find it useful to batch STARS Slips and then log out at the end of the day.
