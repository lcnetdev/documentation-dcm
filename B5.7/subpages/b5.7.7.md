The subject cataloger may receive items from either his/her own team or from a different
team (cross-team cataloging; the descriptive aspect has been done elsewhere). In all cases the
subject cataloger records information only on the STARS Slip generated in his/her own team,
which carries the team's name in the header at the top of the slip. The information recorded on
the STARS slip varies slightly depending upon whether single- or cross-team cataloging is done.
