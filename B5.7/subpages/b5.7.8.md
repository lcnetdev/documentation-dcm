The team member doing this aspect records/checks data on the STARS Slip as follows:
**Statistics area:** Records in *arabic numerals* the number of bibliographic records changed as
the result of shelflisting and the number of items reshelflisted.
*Completion area:*

1. records in *arabic numerals* the title and volume count for the item shelflisted;
2. indicates the appropriate source category (see DCM B5.7.5.3.b above) for NCCP
adapts and for some items received in LC Washington for shelflisting only, e.g., some
items from an LC overseas office; this is done by the person doing the shelflisting
aspect because it is the first opportunity to indicate source;
3. does a final check to see that all data on the STARS Slip appear to be correct for the
item as it leaves the team;
4. records the date of completion;
5. removes the STARS Slip and routes it for logging out;
6. forwards the item out of the team.
