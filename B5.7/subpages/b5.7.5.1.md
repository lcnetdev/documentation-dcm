The top area is designed to display all information known at the time the item is logged into
STARS: the team accepting the item, the RID, intended cataloging activity, language of the item,
date logged in, cataloging priority, and bibliographic type. These data are all input when the item
is logged in; they are printed out at the top of the slip for convenience in shelving and identifying
items as they await cataloging. Catalogers and technicians occasionally manually change the
information in this area of the slip, for instance to correct errors in logging in or when the
priority of an item is upgraded in connection with establishing a numbered series (cf. DCM
C11.2.4).
