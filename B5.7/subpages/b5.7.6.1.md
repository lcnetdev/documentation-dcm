The team member doing the descriptive aspect records/checks data on the STARS Slip as
follows:
*Top area:* Ensures that the data at the top of the slip are correct. For teams receiving items
not yet under initial bibliographic control, this may involve writing in the RID. The priority and
language may also need to be corrected manually on the slip.
*Statistics area:* Records in **arabic** numerals the number of name authorities made/modified
and the number of bibliographic records changed.
*Completion area:*

a. records the date of completion if the cataloging activity is MLC or CIP
verification/upgrade;
b. indicates, as necessary, the appropriate source category (see DCM B5.7.5.3.b above)
for items receiving full cataloging, new input from ONC/PREMARC (note that the
new input procedure is stimulated by added volume activity and therefore the item
involved will have been logged-in as such), MLC, and collection level cataloging;
c. indicates, as necessary, the activity completed;
*Call number, etc.:*
a. if the item is to receive subject cataloging in the same team, provides, in the
case of items classified as a collection, the relevant information in the call number
and collected set elements;
