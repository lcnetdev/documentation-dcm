VIDEORECORDINGS
Fill out a Materials Routing slip to send the book to the Music Bibliographic Access Section.
Both the book and the accompanying videorecording should be sent to MBAS.  If the videorecording is
not securely attached to the main resource, do so with a rubber band.  Place the materials on the
appropriate shelf in the division’s outgoing distribution area. For a list of ABA division distribution areas,
see http://www.loc.gov/staff/aba/psd/movement.pdf.
