Miniature books are processed on the appropriate team in the Cataloging Directorate in the same
way as any other book (with the addition of the data prescribed in B15.5 below) up to and
including shelflisting (cf. CSM G 810; CSM G 620). Once shelflisting is complete, place the
item on the appropriate shelf in the Special Materials Cataloging Division for forwarding to
RBSCD.
Miniature books that have been ordered by RBSCD or selected as rare by RBSCD are cataloged
by the Rare Book Cataloging Team in the Special Materials Cataloging Division.
