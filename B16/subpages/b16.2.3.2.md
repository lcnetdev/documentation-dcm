Value 8 in Leader/17 is needed to identify records in the CIP state until CIP verification takes
place. Therefore, only 042 = pcc is available to identify CIP records that will become LC core
level records. CIP staff will add 042 = pcc to their prompt screens so that records in a CIP in-
process state will already contain the core level identification when the material enters the
cataloging stream. If and when an individual item is done at full level, it is necessary at the point
of descriptive cataloging to remove the 042 field.
