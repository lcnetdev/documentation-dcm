Complete/check the content designation for the fields associated with the descriptive aspect.
**a. Variable fields**
The variable fields associated with the descriptive aspect are all those in the record not
otherwise associated with the subject, shelflisting, or decimal classification aspects.
Exclusive of decimal classification, "who" actually does "what" will depend upon a variety
of factors in effect at the team level.
