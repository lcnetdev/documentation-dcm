*Collections Services Memorandum* No. 128 ( *Centralized and Dispersed Cataloging in*
*Collections Services* ) states the division of cataloging responsibilities between the Cataloging
Directorate and units in the Public Service and Collection Management I Directorate. Within the
Cataloging Directorate cataloging is done by the following five cataloging divisions, generally
oriented to subject/form of material:
Arts and Sciences Cataloging Division (ASCD)
History and Literature Cataloging Division (HLCD)
Regional and Cooperative Cataloging Division (RCCD)
Social Sciences Cataloging Division (SSCD)
Special Materials Cataloging Division (SMCD)
In addition, the Enhanced Cataloging Division does a form of LC copy cataloging (identified as
"lccopycat" in an 922 field in the bibliographic record and therefore sometimes referred to as
*Note* that as stated in the memorandum cited above, serials are done in the Serial Record
Division.
