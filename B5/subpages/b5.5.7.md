It is often necessary to record information relevant to the cataloging process itself.
**a. Permanent**
Record information that is permanent (e.g., "Complete in 3 v.") in the 952 field according
the guidelines stated in DCM B9. This information is available only in the LC machine
record, i.e., the information is not distributed.  This field is also used for unobtrusive quality
control (the field contains the word "Online") and when so used that is generally the first 952
field in the record. See the guidelines in MUMS Field Order Tables (Input/Update).
**b. Temporary**
Whether and how to record information that is temporary is at the discretion of the team.
