The ability to physically locate materials in process is fundamental for several reasons,
including the basic requirement of service to the Library's constituencies. This requirement is
met by a procedure called "charging" (sometimes "955 charging"). A separate and distinct
activity in support of various aspects of the management of cataloging is done through the
Statistical Reporting System (STARS). Use of this system is done through a procedure called
"logging" (sometimes "STARS logging"). See DCM B5.6 (Appendix 1) for the conventions
used in charging and logging.
