4. names of families, e.g., Adams family (600 field with a second indicator value of "3");
5. topical subject headings with subject subdivisions;
6. name headings with non-free-floating subject subdivisions;
7. general and chronological subdivisions from the various instructions in SHM on
free-floating subdivisions (e.g., H 1095, etc.), including the category of headings under
which each subdivision may be used and a citation to the particular free-floating list in
SHM on which the particular subdivision appears.
Use of *Classification Web* as recommended in these guidelines assumes that the searcher has
taken the COIN (formerly IDTD) course “Classification Web: Introduction to Searching LCC.”
