When importing an RDA record from OCLC, Z-Processor will recognize the encoding level
and language of cataloging of the record and will generally identify the correct cataloging
stream:
• **OrigRes** –indicates that the record is a lower-level record and/or the language
of cataloging is other than English.  Process the record as RDA.
