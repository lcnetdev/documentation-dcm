When importing a record from OCLC, Z-Processor will recognize the encoding level and
language of cataloging of the record and will generally identify the correct cataloging stream:
• **OrigRes** –indicates that the record is a lower-level record, and/or the language
of cataloging is other than English.  Process the record as RDA.
• **OrigCop** --indicates that an existing LC AACR2 record for another edition can
be used to create a new RDA record.
