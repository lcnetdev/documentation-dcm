The Copy Cataloging Manual (CCM) draws together within the *Descriptive Cataloging*
*Manual* the information and guidelines applicable to **all** aspects of LC copy cataloging, although
these aspects go beyond those of descriptive cataloging itself.  The CCM is oriented toward
books.  Documentation relevant to other forms of material may be made available, usually by the
Policy and Standards Division (ABA/POLICY) in consultation with the appropriate units.  The
manual assumes that staff performing copy cataloging have received classroom and/or
one-on-one training in the aspects of copy cataloging review for which they are responsible.
