A record created prior to 1982 may contain the obsolete value blank (Undefined) in indicator
position 2 of field 050.  Change it to “0" (Assigned by LC).
A record created before the implementation of the ILS on August 16, 1999 may contain one
or more 050 fields with some variety of “IN PROCESS” or “BOOK NOT YET IN LC” legend.
Delete all 050 fields except the one ultimately used for the LC call number.
