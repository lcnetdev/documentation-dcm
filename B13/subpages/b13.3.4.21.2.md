If a U.S. publisher or distributor is appropriate to the resource being cataloged but is lacking
in the imported record, add it according to LC practice (LCRI 1.4D4).  Change/do not change
existing data as follows:

1. do not change to a fuller or shorter form–accept as found in the imported record;
Examples:
"Rowe" vs. "D.N. Rowe"
"The Entomological Society of Canada" vs. "Entomological Society of Canada"
"Western Publishing" vs. "Western Publishing Company"
2. do not substitute an abbreviated form for a spelled out form;
Examples:
accept: Western Publishing
do not Western Pub.
substitute:
accept: Government Printing Office
do not G.P.O.
substitute:
3. do not change “[s.n.]” to a probable publisher.
