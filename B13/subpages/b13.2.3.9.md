With respect to series, although LC series policy since June 1, 2006 is not to provide
controlled access points for series in LC original cataloging, accept the series fields as given in
records used for copy cataloging (cf. DCM M5.1.2.2).  Do not, however, search such series to
determine if they are represented by an SAR.
With respect to works of literary authors, determine whether the author's authority record
contains a literary author number from the LC classification scheme; if so, note it for use at the
classification step.
