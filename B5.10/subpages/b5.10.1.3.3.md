The following are **never** considered to be translations:
Editions of the Bible
Bibliographies, catalogs (including exhibition catalogs), indexes, and
guides to literature
Language dictionaries
Language editions with no evidence of a language of translation
Separate but simultaneous initial publications of multilingual countries
or institutions, regardless of whether a uniform title has been
assigned.
EXAMPLE: United Nations publication in French. The same work
has also been published separately in English, in Italian,
in Spanish, and in Russian.
