*predominance apparent for any one of the original languages*
Treat as a translation.  In field 041, record the code for the text of the
translation in subfield $a and the code “mul” (multilingual) in subfield $h.
EXAMPLE: An anthology of poems translated into English from eight Indic
languages.
Input:  041 1# $a eng $h mul
Pre-Nov. 1989 practice: 041 1# $a engmul
